# Donations FAQ

This is a starter draft. HSO leadership must verify and complete this content before agent use.

## Summary

HSO accepts donations via PayPal. Donations support our language classes, job training, counseling, and community programs serving refugees and immigrants in Trondheim.

## How do I donate?

The easiest way: visit paypal.me/HopeSpotDonation and choose any amount you'd like to give.

If PayPal doesn't work for you, just reply and we'll figure out an alternative — bank transfer or another method.
